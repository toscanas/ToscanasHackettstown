About Us

Italian Restaurant

Our Story

Hailing from Arthur Avenue in the Bronx, we pride ourselves in authentic Italian cuisine. Toscana Trattoria opened in 1992 on Main Street in the Peapack-Gladstone area. We continued our legacy in Chester, New Jersey in1997. Our family-run establishment maintains the Toscanas heritage on Main Street in Hackettstown delivering fresh local ingredients including homemade bread, pasta and desserts. We also have whole wheat, vegetarian-vegan options and welcome everyone into our restaurant to experience what Toscana Trattoria has to offer. When you’re here, you’re family!
